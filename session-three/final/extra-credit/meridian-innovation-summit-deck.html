Reel 06 · Risks WHAT COULD GO WRONG
RISKS & MITIGATIONS

Four things to watch before we lock the schedule.

Risk 01 Speaker concentration Five companies supply 22% of proposals. Cap any single company at three speakers across the program.
Risk 02 Leadership depth Only 4 strong leadership submissions. Curate 2 keynote slots and recruit 2 more by closed invitation.
Risk 03 Out-of-window proposal One submission targets a date outside the event. Decline politely, suggest a virtual session, preserve the relationship.
Risk 04 AV & demo load AI-in-Production talks lean on live demos. Confirm bandwidth, backup recordings, and run-of-show rehearsal two days prior.
